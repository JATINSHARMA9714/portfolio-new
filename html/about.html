About

I,m Jatin.A Competitive Programmer, Web Developer and Problem Solver

Hello! I'm Jatin Sharma, a final year Computer Science and Engineering undergraduate at Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra. With a passion for coding and problem-solving, I've honed my skills in various aspects of web development.

I specialize in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js), enabling me to build dynamic and responsive web applications. My academic experience has been a perfect blend of theory and practice, allowing me to understand complex concepts and apply them effectively in real-world scenarios.

One of the aspects of web development I enjoy the most is creating cool animations for modern websites. I love how animations can bring a website to life, making it not only visually appealing but also enhancing user experience. Leveraging CSS, JavaScript, and various animation libraries, I strive to create smooth, engaging, and interactive animations that captivate users.

Additionally, I sometimes love designing components of a website from scratch. The process of crafting aesthetically pleasing and functional components and then integrating them seamlessly into a project is incredibly rewarding for me. This passion for both design and development drives me to continually explore new techniques and tools, ensuring that my web applications are not only functional but also delightful to use.

This is one of my favourite quotes:-

“There is nothing so useless as doing efficiently that which should not be done at all.”

This resonates so deeply with me because I have no desire to make pretty things just for the sake of it. Some people love that! But it's not for me. I only want to make, design or build ideas and products that are real, that matter and have an impact in the world.
